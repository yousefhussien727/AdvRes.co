About Advanced Resources co.

Advanced Resources Company was established in 1423 and began as an establishment under the name Scope for Metal Production, which specialized in metal fabrication such as manufacturing warehouses, fences, umbrellas, and various types of metal decorative works

In 1434, Mohammed Azouz Al-Jazairi Foundation - SCOPE - was transformed to the new name "Advanced Resources Company" as one of the manufacturing branches in Medina - Saudi Arabia, which includes a department for stainless steel works and a department for aluminum works.
